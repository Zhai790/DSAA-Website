When you donate to the Disabled Sailing Association of Alberta, you provide support and opportunity for people with disabilities to benefit from the therapeutic and rehabilitative nature of sailing.

Contribute any amount you choose easily and securely.

Financial Accountability And Transparency

As a registered charity, The Disabled Sailing Association of Alberta is dedicated to financial accountability and transparency.

Our Association maintains a budget and completes an annual review of financial controls and processes. In addition, the board reviews all financial transactions to ensure the financial records are complete and accurate.
